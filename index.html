Gives customers the possibility to group in order to make deals. Imagine 10 of you want to go to a restaurant, would it not be nice to choose a restaurant that is willing to discount because there are ten of you? Customers can enter search topics to find already existing circles or create themselves in order to get reduced prices Merchants can state the amount they are willing to discount along a lower bound for participants

You want to make a trip and there are 20 of you? Come to the bargainer app and make your wish. The listening merchants will get to you.
